Have a back yard?

You should have Chickens!


Why on Earth would I want chickens when I can get a dozen eggs for $2?

  1. Many (but not all) breeds of chicken make friendly, funny pets.
  2. Free-range eggs look and taste better (and are probably more nutritious).
  3. Chickens take care of fertilizing your yard and eat a lot of bugs.

Okay, but aren't they a lot of work and expense?

They're no more difficult or expensive than a cat, and when's the last time your cat fed you?!
